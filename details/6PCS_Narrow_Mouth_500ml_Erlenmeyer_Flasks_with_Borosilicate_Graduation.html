Precision Meets Durability in Science

These 6-piece Erlenmeyer flasks are designed for scientists and students who demand accuracy and reliability in their work. With a 500ml capacity, they are the perfect choice for a variety of lab experiments, chemical reactions, and scientific processes. Crafted from high-quality 3.3 borosilicate glass, these flasks are built to withstand extreme temperature changes and resist chemical corrosion, making them long-lasting and safe for use in demanding environments.

The clear printed graduation markings offer precise volume readings, ensuring consistency in your experiments. The large marking spots enhance usability, whether you're working in a well-lit lab or a dimly lit workspace. The narrow mouth design is ideal for adding liquids or powders with minimal spillage, providing a practical and professional solution for your lab needs.
