24 Gallon Flammable Cabinet for Secure Chemical Storage

Designed with safety and durability in mind, this 24-gallon flammable cabinet offers a dependable solution for storing flammable materials in any environment. Its fireproof structure and heavy-duty steel build ensure reliable performance in workshops, garages, laboratories, and more.

Key Features

  • Fireproof construction with 2mm thick double-layer cold-rolled steel for superior protection

  • Built-in static grounding conductor to prevent electrostatic discharge hazards

  • High load-bearing capacity, ideal for heavy-duty and industrial use

Whether you're a homeowner, business owner, or industrial professional, this flammable cabinet is the perfect fit for your safety needs.
