Juexica Plastic Strip Door Curtain

These Juexica plastic strip door curtains are made from high-quality, durable PVC material that is smooth, waterproof, and scratch-resistant. Designed for long-lasting use, they offer a reliable solution for maintaining temperature control in your space.

The clear design allows visibility inside your doors without having to open them, making these curtains perfect for warehouses, freezers, and commercial environments. They effectively block dust, reduce noise, and help prevent cold air from escaping, ensuring energy efficiency without compromising accessibility.

With a thickness of just 0.08'', these curtains provide excellent insulation, especially during the colder months. The easy installation with included screws and hardware makes them a convenient option for any commercial door, and their flexibility allows for simple trimming to fit your specific needs.

  • Durable and waterproof PVC material that resists wear and tear for long-term use.
  • Easy to install with pre-punched holes and mounting hardware, saving you time and effort.
  • Clear design for visibility while keeping your space secure and preventing the entry of dust and cold air.
  • Designed for low temperature environments and outdoor use, ensuring performance in tough conditions.
  • Versatile application for a wide range of commercial, industrial, and cold storage doors.

Whether you're looking for a waterproof door curtain, a clear PVC door curtain, or an industrial door curtain that offers both insulation and visibility, these Juexica curtains are the perfect choice. They provide a practical and reliable way to enhance your workspace's energy efficiency and comfort.
